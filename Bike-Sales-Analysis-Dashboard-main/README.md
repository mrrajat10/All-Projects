# Bike-Sales-Analysis-Dashboard

## 1. Title-Bike-Sales-Analysis-Dashboard

## 2. Executive Summary & Impact

-This project presents a dynamic, interactive Excel dashboard designed to analyze customer demographics, purchase behavior, and regional bike sales performance for Bike Sales.

The dashboard empowers stakeholders to:

-Understand customer conversion patterns.

-Identified high-value customer groups.

-Compare buyer vs. non-buyer behavior.

-Evaluate regional market performance.

-Make data-driven decisions on marketing & sales strategy

### Impact: The dashboard transforms raw customer data into actionable insights, enabling faster decision-making and improved sales forecasting.

## 3. Buisness Problem

Bike Sales required a clear, consolidated view of:

-Which customer groups are most likely to buy a bike?

-How do age, gender, income, and region affect buying behavior?

-What external factors (commute distance, marital status, cars owned, children count) influence purchase decisions?

-Which regions perform best in terms of bike sales?

-What business strategies can help improve overall customer conversion?

#### The goal was to create a clean, interactive dashboard that provides leadership with instant, real-time insights.

## 6. Results

### Key Results

-Conversion Rate: ~48%

-Gender Split: Male vs Female buyers almost equal(51% Male vs 49% Female)

-High-Buying Age Group: Adults

-Top Regions: North America & Europe lead sales

-Occupation Influence: Skilled manual & professional roles buy more bikes

-Income Impact: Higher income positively correlates with purchase

-Commute Distance Insight: Short-distance commuters show stronger buying patterns

## 7. Buisness Recommendations

-Target Adults (20-50 years) with tailored marketing campaigns.

-Boost promotions in top-buying regions (North America, Europe).

-Create occupation-based offers for Skilled Manual & Professional workers.

-Provide premium financing schemes to attract higher-income customers.

-Personalize ads using demographics like marital status, cars owned, and children count.

-Leverage commute-friendly bikes for customers with <5 miles travel distance

-Increase engagement with female customers as female conversion rate is high
